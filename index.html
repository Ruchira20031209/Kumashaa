Our Story

Kumashaa Grocery started as a small neighborhood shop in 2000, founded by husband-wife Ajith and Pushpa. What began as a single aisle of essential goods has grown into the community hub you see today - but our heart remains the same.

During our first year, a severe storm left our neighborhood without power for days. While bigger stores closed, we kept our doors open by candlelight, running a manual ledger and sharing our generator with elderly neighbors. Word spread, and soon locals were bringing us homemade preserves in thanks. That's when we realized - we weren't just selling groceries, we were nourishing relationships.
